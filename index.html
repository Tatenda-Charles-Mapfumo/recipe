Tatenda's Recipe For The Best Rice and Beef Stew

Ingredients

For The Beef Stew:

  • 10 Ounces of Cut Beef
  • Beef and Onion Soup (Powder)
  • 1 Ounce Onion
  • 1 Ounce Tomato
  • Half of 1 Ounce Green Pepper

For The Rice:

  • 4 Cups of Rice

Instructions:

For the Rice

  • Put the 4 cups of rice in a pot to boil, for about 45 mins to an hour, your rice should be ready afer.

For the Beef Stew

  • Boil the beef for about 30 to 45 minutes on medium to high heat
  • Cut the onions, tomatoes and green pepper and fry them for about 5 minutes
  • Add the Beef and Onion Soup
  • Finally add the boiled beef, and stir for about 3 minutes

Serving is for 3 to 4 people
